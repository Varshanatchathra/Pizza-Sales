# Pizza-Sales-Data(Interactive Dashboard creation using Power BI and SQL for verifying KPI requirements)
## Project Objective
- A Pizza store needs to analyze what are the measures to be taken for improving the sales.By analyzing the top and bottom sellers based on the Total revenue ,total quantity and total orders of pizza we can focus on how we can improve the business.

## Dataset used:
- <a href="https://github.com/Varshanatchathra/Pizza-Sales/blob/main/pizza%20SALes.pbix">Dataset</a>
## Problem statement
- Percentage of sales by Pizza size
- Total pizzas sold by Pizza category
- Top 5 Best sellers by Revenue,Total quantity and Total orders
- Bottom 5 Best sellers by Revenue,Total quantity and Total orders

## Process
- Imported the data into Power BI for analysis and visualizations.
- Loaded the dataset into Power query editor- verified column names and data types.
- Performed data cleaning operations like Handling missing values,Removing duplicate records and data type correction.
- Performed validation to ensure accuracy, cross checked totals such as Total quantity sold  and total revenue.
- Created calculated columns and measures using DAX such as: Total Revenue,Average pizza per order,Total orders ,.etc
- Created interactive dashboards in Power BI including Top & Bottom Selling pizzas,Daily & monthly trends,Busiest Days,Sales performance.

## Project Insight
- By analyzing daily trend friday is the day which received highest number of orders with total orders of 3538.
- By analyzing monthly trend July is the month which received highest number of orders with total orders of 1935.
- Percentage of sales by Pizza sales and Pizza category is visualized by Donut charts.
- Total pizzas sold by pizza category is visualized by funnel,classic Pizza contributes the most.
- Best Sellers are The Thai Chicken pizza Contributes to maximum Revenue,The Classic Deluxe Pizza Contributes to maximum Total Quantities,
The Classic Deluxe Pizza contributes to maximum Total Orders.
- Worst sellers are The Brie Carre pizza Contributes to minimum Revenue,The Brie Carre Pizza Contributes to minimum Total Quantities,
The Brie Carre Pizza contributes to minimum Total Order.


  ## Final Conclusion:
  To improve the Pizza sales, a strategic marketing plan should be focused on the days which has low sales by introducing combo offers or discounts .The Pizza category which contributes most should be highly promoted which attracts customers.Large pizza contributes the highest percentage of sales so We can offer family combos on friday and saturday evenings which holds higher orders.
